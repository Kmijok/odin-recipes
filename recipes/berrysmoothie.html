Berry Smoothie

The perfect mix of refreshing flavours and healthy vitamins while being a simple and easy cool drink

What you'll need
  • Some bananas
  • Berries of your choice
  • Milk, Fruit Juice or Yoghurt
  • a Mixer
Here's how you do it
  1. Take out your mixer of choice
  2. Prepare your berries. Frozen berries do not need to be heated
  3. Add berries and fruits into mixer according to taste. Place larger chunks (e.g. bananas) closest to the blade
  4. Add a little bit of liquid to help the blades. After mixing a bit, add more and mix until you reach a desired consistency
  5. Pour into a glass and enjoy

You can also add other fruits such as mango or pineapple. For a health-boost, you can toss in some seeds or oats. No sugar needed, serve immediately.
