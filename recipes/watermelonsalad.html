Watermelon Cucumber Salad

This simple, yet oh so tasty salad will leave you more refreshed than you can imagine. I bet you haven't even thought of the combo before

What you'll need for this delicious treat:
  • Watermelon
  • Cucumber
  • Feta cheese
  • Mint
  • Optional: A drizzle of olive oil
Here's what you need to do to end up with a bowl o' deliciousness
  1. Dice the watermelon
  2. Dice the cucumber
  3. Crumble a little bit of feta cheese over the cucumbers and the melons
  4. Add some mint leaves
  5. Drizzle a few drops of olive oil if you wish to
  6. Mix and enjoy
